Playcar Website
For Horizons Nexus Hackathon. 

Cards are 3 blue house, 3 orange #2, 9 orange drive, 9 red clippy, and another 9 red clippy.

Takes place in a car gps, driving on the way home in San Francisco. There are three location marks that are numbered. 
The first location mark takes you to The Painted Ladies, where you can play tic tac toe with a bot. Instead of X's and O's, there are clips. Click continue to go back home.
The second location mark takes you to the Golden Gate Bridge where you play a memory game, find the clips that are two alike, click continue to go back home
The last location mark is a house. You made it, you are home! Click on the location pin it brings you back into the car.
